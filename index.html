Top three activities to do at Santa Rosa

Go for a ride in the Safari

Safari West is a great place to spend time with family and experience wildlife like you never have before.

Learn about the city by visiting a museum

The Charles M. Schullz Museum features the largest collection of original Peanuts artwork in the world. Afterall, who wouldn't want to spend time Charlie and Snoopy

Have a fun or sweaty time at Epicenter

Poppy Bank Epicenter is an all-in-one destination. You can enjoy activies such as bowling, aracade, laser tag, the gym, and many more activites.
